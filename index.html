About Ladakh

Ladakh, often called the "Land of High Passes," is a region in northern India known for its stunning landscapes, Buddhist monasteries, and unique culture. From the azure waters of Pangong Lake to the sand dunes of Nubra Valley, Ladakh offers a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Best time to visit: May to September
